Convenient Location

We are located in the heart of the city, making it easy for you to stop by for a haircut or beard trim on your lunch break or after work. We are open six days a week and offer appointment scheduling to fit your busy schedule.

Welcome to Barber.io, your premier destination for all of your barbering needs. Our shop has a rich history dating back to the early 20th century, when our founder first opened the doors to serve the community with top-notch haircuts and grooming services.

Over the years, Barber.io has become a beloved institution in the community, known for our skilled barbers, comfortable atmosphere, and commitment to providing the best customer experience possible. From classic cuts to modern styles, our team is equipped to handle any haircut you desire.

In addition to haircuts, we also offer a variety of other services such as beard trims, hot towel shaves, and facial grooming. Whatever your grooming needs may be, our team is ready to give you the royal treatment. We hope to see you soon at Barber.io, where we look forward to giving you the haircut you deserve.
